You can join our live translations here

Just choose hall and desired language below to start

You may also connect with your Desktop or Mobile Mumble client: connect to live.c3lingo.org port 64738, and choose the desired channel. To test your client, go to the "test" channel

Hall-0 is the original audio, hall-1 and hall-2 are the interpreted versions. Hall-1 is usually English or German, depending on the language of the talk, while hall-2 will be French, Spanish, Mandarin, or even Schwyzerdütsch. Follow @c3lingo on Twitter to learn about upcoming live interpretations and the languages offered.
